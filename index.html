What’s going on here

Amplified is a scripted survival SMP — staff plan fights, betrayals, big moments, and story events ahead of time. Not everything is scripted though, and that’s kind of the deal. Your choices still shape things. The world just happens to have writers in it.

We run on Paper with Aikar’s flags on dedicated hardware, support both Java and Bedrock through Geyser (same IP, same world, no extra setup on your end), and use CoreProtect to log every block. If someone touches your stuff, we know. Grief gets rolled back; griefers get removed.

500 player slots. Server-wide events when the story calls for it. TPS isn’t always perfect but we actually care about it, which puts us ahead of most servers this size.
