Server Guidelines

Rules

Read carefully before joining. Ignorance of the rules is not an excuse. When in doubt, ask staff first.

✅ General Rules
G1Be respectful and kind to other players out of character (OOC). No toxicity, targeted insults, excessive sarcasm meant to hurt feelings, or public arguments.
G2No harassment, hate speech, discrimination, or real-life threats of any kind.
G3Keep all content PG-13. No excessive swearing, sexual topics, extreme gore outside scripted scenes, or offensive builds/skins/names.
G4Staff decisions about story, scenes, and rule enforcement are final. No arguing in public — appeal privately if needed.
G5When in doubt, ask staff first.
🎮 In-Game (Scripted) Rules
IG1This is a scripted SMP. Some fights, deaths, events, builds, and items are pre-planned by staff/directors.
IG2Do not interfere with active scenes. Stay at least 100 blocks away unless explicitly part of the scene. Do not build, mine, make noise, or cause mobs/entities to enter the area. If you accidentally enter, leave immediately.
IG3Only use mods, texture packs, or clients explicitly approved by staff. Using anything else = immediate ban.
IG4Duped or exploited items are only allowed if staff gave them for a specific plot point. Using them otherwise = removal + punishment.
IG5No griefing, stealing, PvP, or destruction outside of staff-approved scripted events. This includes breaking blocks, taking items from chests/shulkers, killing players or pets, or blowing things up — even if it looks "minor" or "unclaimed."
IG6Staged betrayals or deaths only happen when the director tells you to.
IG7Staff can reset, rewind, rollback, or remove anything (builds, items, deaths) that breaks the story. Story comes first.
IG8No spamming in chat, voice, or with commands/actions.
🎥 Story & Content Rules
S1Follow your assigned role (ally, villain, neutral, mystery, etc.) to the best of your ability. Major deviations need staff approval.
S2Do not leak future plot points, scripts, or behind-the-scenes info — in-game or on Discord.
S3Deaths, item losses, betrayals, and stashes can be staged/faked for the story — don't assume everything is real.
S4Cooperate with reshoot or "camera" requests so creators can get good footage.
S5Story > Chaos. Fun chaotic moments are allowed only if they don't ruin planned story beats or other players' scenes.
💬 Discord Rules
D1Keep In-Character (IC) and Out-of-Character (OOC) chat in the correct channels. No IC talk in OOC channels and vice versa.
D2No NSFW content, memes, images, or discussions anywhere.
D3No doxxing, sharing personal information, or real-life drama.
D4No spamming messages, emojis, caps, or repeatedly pinging staff/members.
D5Do not ask to be unmuted or unbanned in DMs or public channels — use the proper appeal process.
D6Speak English only in all public channels (voice and text).
D7Be respectful — no backseat directing, arguing about staff decisions, or toxicity.
⚡ Performance Rules
P1No lag machines or excessive redstone contraptions (flying machines, large item sorters, rapid clocks, or huge auto-farms). Keep farms small and efficient.
P2No more than 30 of any single mob type in one area. Staff may cull excess mobs without warning.
P3No chunk loaders, permanent portals, or anything that keeps many chunks loaded 24/7.
P4Avoid massive structures with thousands of blocks (especially lighting, leaves, or water) in one area.
P5If the server lags or crashes because of your build/farm, staff will ask you to simplify or remove it. Repeated issues = temporary removal of the build.
P6Report major lag sources to staff immediately instead of exploiting them.
