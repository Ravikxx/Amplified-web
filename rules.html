Rules

Read these before you join. Ignorance isn’t an excuse, and “I didn’t know” won’t save you from a ban. When in doubt, ask staff in Discord before doing the thing.

General
1Be respectful out of character. No targeted insults, ongoing toxicity, or public arguments. Banter is fine; harassment isn’t.
2No hate speech, discrimination, or real-life threats. Zero tolerance, permanent ban.
3Keep content PG-13. No excessive swearing, sexual topics, extreme gore outside scripted scenes, or offensive builds, skins, or names.
4Staff decisions on story and enforcement are final. If you disagree, appeal privately — not in public chat.
5If you’re unsure whether something is allowed, ask staff first.
In-Game
1This is a scripted SMP. Some fights, deaths, events, and items are pre-planned. That’s not a bug, it’s the whole premise.
2Don’t walk into active scenes. Stay 100+ blocks away unless you’re supposed to be there. If you stumble in by accident, leave immediately — don’t stick around to watch.
3Only use mods, texture packs, or clients explicitly approved by staff. Anything else is an immediate ban, no exceptions.
4No griefing, stealing, or PvP outside of staff-approved scripted events. Even if something looks unclaimed or minor — don’t touch it without permission.
5Duped or exploited items are only allowed if staff gave them to you for a specific plot reason. Otherwise they’ll be removed and you’ll be punished.
6Staged betrayals or deaths only happen when the director gives the signal. Do not go off-script.
7Staff can reset, rewind, or rollback anything that breaks the story. Story comes first. Your progress is not exempt.
8No spamming in chat, voice, or with commands. Includes emote spam.
Story & Roles
1Play your assigned role to the best of your ability — ally, villain, neutral, whatever it is. Major deviations need staff approval.
2Do not leak future plot points, scripts, or behind-the-scenes info — in-game, on Discord, or anywhere else.
3Deaths, item losses, and betrayals may be staged. Don’t assume everything is real — it’s part of the story.
4Cooperate when staff ask for a reshoot or camera angle. Getting good footage matters.
5Unscripted chaos is only fine if it doesn’t wreck a planned story beat or ruin someone else’s scene.
Remember: not everyone knows what’s scripted and what isn’t — that ambiguity is intentional. Don’t break the immersion for others.
Discord
1Keep IC (in-character) and OOC (out-of-character) chat in the right channels. They exist for a reason.
2No NSFW content anywhere — images, links, discussions, memes, none of it.
3No doxxing or sharing anyone’s personal information.
4Don’t spam — messages, emojis, caps, or pings. One message is enough.
5If you’re muted or banned, use the proper appeal process. Don’t DM staff or post about it in public channels.
6English only in public channels (voice and text).
Performance
1No lag machines or excessive redstone — flying machines, large item sorters, rapid clocks, or huge auto-farms. Keep farms small and efficient.
2No more than 30 of any single mob type in one area. Staff may cull excess mobs without warning.
3No chunk loaders, permanent portals, or anything designed to keep lots of chunks loaded at all times.
4If your build tanks the server, staff will ask you to simplify it. If it keeps happening, they’ll remove it.
5Report lag sources to staff instead of exploiting them.
